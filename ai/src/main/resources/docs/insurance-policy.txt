# Accepted Insurance
HealthConnect supports major providers including AIA, Great Eastern, Prudential, Manulife, and NTUC Income, and regional insurers.

# Eligibility
Enter your policy number or upload a photo of your insurance card during registration to verify eligibility.

# Claims
Claims are submitted automatically if your provider is in-network. For out-of-network, downloadable forms are available.

# Privacy Policy
Your data is encrypted and compliant with HIPAA and GDPR standards. Full policy available at /privacy.

# Billing
If insurance is not applicable, billing is handled via the HealthConnect portal. Receipts are emailed after every session.
