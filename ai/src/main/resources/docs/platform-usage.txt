# Registration
To register for HealthConnect, visit our registration page and enter your personal details. You must verify your email to activate the account.

# Declaring Symptoms
Users can declare symptoms using the "Describe Your Condition" section. This helps doctors prepare for your consultation.

# Choosing a Doctor
You can search for doctors by specialty, availability, and user ratings before booking an appointment.

# Booking Appointments
Appointments can be scheduled through the calendar interface. Confirm your preferred time and doctor to complete the booking.

# Payment
HealthConnect supports credit cards, insurance billing, and digital wallets. Payment must be completed before joining a consultation.

# Joining a Consultation
Join virtual consultations through the "My Appointments" tab by clicking the "Join Meeting" button at the scheduled time.
